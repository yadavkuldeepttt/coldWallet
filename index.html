What is a hardware/cold wallet, and why do I need one?
A hardware/cold wallet is a physical device designed to store seed phrase or private keys for cryptocurrencies securely. It provides an offline storage solution, protecting your digital assets from online threats like hacking. Using a hardware/cold wallet adds an extra layer of security to your crypto holdings compared to software wallets.
